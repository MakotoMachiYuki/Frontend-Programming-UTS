About This Item

The everyday sling designed for travel

The Day Sling 3 is a versatile sling bag designed for travel. The spacious main compartment opens wide for improved access and visibility and features multiple pockets for your everyday essentials. On the back, you'll find a secure pocket that keeps your passport and cards safe. Move Smarte.

Features

  • 1680D Cordura® ballistic nylon exterior (bluesign® approved)
  • YKK® zippers
  • Duraflex® plastic hardware
  • Soft woven lining for a premium look and feel (bluesign® approved)
  • Quick-access front pocket with keychain
  • Smart organization for your everyday essentials
  • Secure back pocket for your passport or wallet
  • Adjustable shoulder strap with Fidlock® magnetic fastener

    I liked it, but I didn’t love it. I think if I went back in time, I might have reconsidered the purchase. This critique comes after a two week international trip where it was used day in and day out.

    The reason for this critique comes down to two separate concerns:

    1. The hidden back pocket opening does not cover the entire height of the bag. This creates an opening too small to easily slip in my travel wallet even though the space is plenty large. I have to use multiple hands to adjust the opening to get the wallet to slip in correctly. If the opening zipper was at the top of the bag, just behind the main compartment then this issue would be resolved.

    2. The magnetic clasp is not secure and can easily become undone on its own if struck correctly. During my trip, I had this bag worn as a crossbody when driving a go-kart on city streets. It came undone and luckily landed on a portion of the go-kart preventing the entire bag from being left in the street behind me. To salvage the situation, the bag was quickly thrown behind my back where I pushed all of my weight into it to prevent it from going anywhere. It made for a stressful ride given that I knew the bag contained my passport and other valuables.
